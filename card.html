Cast of Characters | The Game

Accenture + The Alan Turing Institute

Outline

Cast of Characters is a card game for collaborators, highlighting the different personas involved in technology development.

Introduction

Differing perspectives can be one of the greatest barriers to ethical & seamless integration of AI tools. During a collaboration with the Alan Turing Institute we developed this interactive card game for a responsible AI module. The game aimed to help innovators understand how different personalities can influence the creation of emerging technologies. I helped to develop the concept and personas for this game, as part of a wider team. I was fully responsible for the illustration and visual language, using GenAI as a co-pilot to help iterate from my initial sketches. I collaborated with a developer to make the final output.

Science Communication

Tech Adoption

Accenture Ireland

Outline

Tech Adoption is a playful exploration of how technologies become ubiquitous, and how that impacts us.

Process

Building on a research project which highlighted the cognitive barriers associated with technology adoption. I created a Vox-style explainer which condensed the findings into a 3 minute video. I collaborated to write the script, flim interviews with the research team and record the narration. After which I moved forward to edit the content and design the motion graphics treatment for the final deliverable. The video was presented to a variety of Accenture clients during the course of the project, which helped them to engage with our research methodologies and core purpose.

Integrated Design

Pathfinder

Accenture Ireland

Introduction

This short term creative project was initially briefed as a branded email, after researching the project background I decided to squeeze the brief a little. These are real challenges with real solutions, so why not situate them in physical space. I relied on a wayfinding motif, printing a selection of signs which acted as a tangible prompt for the audience to engage. I made each sign a viewfinder, allowing the environment to peek through and communicate a sense of focus.

Website

Following on from the launch of the campaign, I was tasked with adapting the identity into a website to socialise the findings from the project. Adapting a physical motif - namely that of a sign - was a unique challenge, and meant translating three dimensional shapes and sensibilities into a 2D format. I kept my approach simple, pulling the curved shapes and colours from the organisational piece and allowing the rest of the design to breathe.
